WHEREAS, to earn the rank of Eagle Scout, a young person must earn Merit Badges in areas including First Aid, Citizenship in the Community, Citizenship in the Nation, Citizenship in the World, Communication, Personal Fitness, Emergency Preparedness/Lifesaving, Environmental Science/Sustainability, Personal Management, Camping, Swimming/Hiking/Cycling, Family Life, and Cooking; and WHEREAS, this distinct honor requires fulfilling requirements in the areas of leadership, service, and outdoor preparedness, as well as advancing through the ranks of Scout, Tenderfoot, Second Class, First Class, Star, and Life; and WHEREAS, this achievement carries with it a special significance both within scouting and beyond, as a Scout moves on to higher education, business or industry, and the world at large; and WHEREAS, the following 79 Delawareans achieved the rank of Eagle Scout in 2024: Bruce Abbott Nicholas Ables James Adair James Andrews Dominick Ayars Samantha Bargren Shane Bennett James Blair Gabriel Bowman Ian Campbell Aiden Casey Cayleb Catherman John Chakwin Nathan Chiou Joseph Clark Breandan Connor Gabriel Craig Gabriel Cruz Braden Curcio Christopher Dean Matthew Demes Nicholas DeNisco Abigail Dilworth Ian Fahnoe Ethan Fink Ian Garcia Matthew Good Victor Goodhue Jr. David Green Christian Guckenberger Logan Haas David Hamilton Albert Hayes Evan Hawley Aidan Hawley Dylan Henry Gavin Isaacs Andrew Isherwood Alexander Isherwood Vincent Jackson Tristan Jenkins Grason Jess Xander Johnson Andrew Kaser Cayla Kleinmeulman Peyton Kott Jacobs Laws Artus David Lawson II Cameron Lesniczak Adelyn Lichtenstein Carter Lunsford Isaiah Matthew Ryan McBride James McCullin Edward Molengraft Austin Myhre Travis Netting Edwin Noon Daniel Pagano Nicholas Parsons Alexander Ralsten Andrew Ramsey Justin Reed Cooper Richle Nicholas Riggi Enrique Rivera Rian Salter Andrew Shahan Jonathan Smith Jonathan Sunde Kyle Surguy Jai Thacker Jack Thomas Jack Thornton Dominick Volpe Justin Webster Connor Wiedmann Colby Wilson Matthew Zoccolillo NOW, THEREFORE: BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the 153rd General Assembly of the State of Delaware, the House of Representatives concurring therein, that the class of 2024 Eagle Scouts is hereby extended congratulations for having attained the highest rank in Scouting America, and our best wishes in their future endeavors.
