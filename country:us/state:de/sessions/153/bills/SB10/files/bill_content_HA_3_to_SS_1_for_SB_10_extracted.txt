Legislation Document SPONSOR: Rep.Â Minor-Brown HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY HOUSE AMENDMENT NO. 3 TO SENATE SUBSTITUTE NO. 1 FOR SENATE BILL NO. 10 AMEND Senate Substitute No. 1 for Senate Bill No. 10 on line 46 by deleting " modification " as it appears therein and inserting in lieu thereof " modification, including the underlying circumstances and nature of the offense, the felony classification of the offense, the length of the underlying sentence, ". SYNOPSIS This Amendment clarifies that in addressing a motion for sentence modification under compassionate release, the court must consider the underlying circumstances and nature of the offense, the felony classification of the offense, and the length of the underlying sentence in addition to the other requirements contained in Senate Substitute No. 1 to Senate Bill No. 10.
