RECOGNIZING THE WEEK OF MAY 11 THROUGH MAY 17, 2025, AS "NATIONAL HOSPITAL WEEK" IN DELAWARE.

WHEREAS, National Hospital Day was established in 1921 by former President Warren G. Harding to be celebrated annually on May 12, the birthday of Florence Nightingale, the founder of modern nursing, to honor her role in revolutionizing hospital care; and

WHEREAS, in 1953, National Hospital Week was established as a time of the year to reflect on the important contribution that hospitals make to provide safe, high-quality health care; and

WHEREAS, there are more than 6,000 hospitals in the United States providing essential and life-saving health care every day in a variety of hospital settings; and

WHEREAS, Delaware hospitals and health systems care for patients through more than 3 million outpatient visits, over 400,000 emergency departments visits, 90,000 inpatient admissions, and in delivering more than 10,000 babies in our state; and
