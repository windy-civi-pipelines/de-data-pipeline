WHEREAS, pets provide companionship, unconditional love, and numerous health benefits to individuals and families throughout the State of Delaware; and WHEREAS, responsible pet ownership is vital to ensuring the well-being of domestic animals and fostering humane communities; and WHEREAS, National Pet Day, observed annually on April 11 th , was founded to recognize the joy that pets bring to our lives and to encourage the adoption of animals in need of loving homes; and WHEREAS, animal shelters and rescue organizations throughout Delaware work tirelessly to provide care, medical attention, and second chances for abandoned and homeless animals; and WHEREAS, the State of Delaware is proud to be recognized as a no-kill state, demonstrating its commitment to the humane treatment and welfare of animals by ensuring that healthy and treatable pets are not euthanized due to lack of space or resources; and WHEREAS, the Joint Committee on Capital Improvement has played a significant role in advancing animal welfare through its support for shelter infrastructure, veterinary services, and programs that promote responsible pet ownership; and WHEREAS, the State of Delaware recognizes the importance of educating the public on the benefits of pet adoption, spaying and neutering, and responsible pet care; and WHEREAS, National Pet Day serves as an opportunity to celebrate the positive impact of pets on individuals and families and to promote efforts that enhance animal welfare.
