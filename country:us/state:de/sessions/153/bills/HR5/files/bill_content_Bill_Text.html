HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY

HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 5

RELATING TO THE ELECTION OF OFFICERS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES.

BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 153 rd General Assembly of the State of Delaware that Sheri J. Knott is elected to serve as Bill Clerk of the House of Representatives at the pleasure of the House of Representatives.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 153 rd General Assembly of the State of Delaware that Dolores Michels is elected to serve as Assistant Bill Clerk of the House of Representatives at the pleasure of the House of Representatives.

SYNOPSIS

This Resolution designates Sheri J. Knott as Bill Clerk of the House of Representatives and Dolores Michels as Assistant Bill Clerk of the House of Representatives to serve at the pleasure of the House of Representatives.
