HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY

HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 13

RECOGNIZING THE MONTH OF MARCH 2025 AS ENDOMETRIOSIS AWARENESS MONTH.

WHEREAS, endometriosis is a disease in which tissue similar to tissue that normally grows in the uterus (the endometrium) grows outside of the uterus; and

WHEREAS, endometriosis is one of the most common gynecological diseases in women and occurs in 1 in 10 women of reproductive age; and

WHEREAS, women experience a delay from three to eleven years between the first symptoms of pain and the final endometriosis diagnosis, which can lead to a lower quality of life and high medical costs; and

WHEREAS, the primary symptoms of endometriosis include pain and infertility, and many with endometriosis live with debilitating, chronic pain; and

WHEREAS, Endometriosis is estimated to be present in 25 percent to 40 percent of women suffering from infertility; and

WHEREAS, approximately 75 percent of women with endometriosis experience a misdiagnosis; and

WHEREAS, the typical timeframe from symptom onset to receiving a diagnosis of endometriosis ranges from 2 to 12 years; and

WHEREAS, Black women and girls tend to be diagnosed with endometriosis when they are 2.6 years older compared to White girls and women and Hispanic girls and women tend to be 3.8 years older compared to White girls and women when they receive a diagnosis of endometriosis; and

WHEREAS, 40 percent of women with endometriosis report impaired career growth due to endometriosis, and approximately 50 percent of women with endometriosis experience a decreased ability to work; and

WHEREAS, medical societies and patient groups have expressed the need for greater public attention and updated resources targeted to public education about this unmet health need for women; and

WHEREAS, there is an ongoing need for additional clinical research and treatment options to manage this debilitating disease.

NOW, THEREFORE:

BE IT RESOLVED by the House of the 153rd General Assembly of the State of Delaware, the Senate concurring therein, that March 2025 shall be known as Endometriosis Awareness Month in Delaware, encouraging state agencies, medical organizations, and community groups to spread awareness, share evidence-based prevention programming and resources, and engage in outreach programs to raise awareness for early detection and treatment of endometriosis, increased education for health care providers, and more culturally competent care.

SYNOPSIS

This House Concurrent Resolution designates March 2025 as Endometriosis Awareness Month in the state of Delaware.
