SYNOPSIS

This Act prohibits discrimination based on weight, height, or body size in transportation, public accommodation, housing, commerce and trade, employment, jury selection, education, and public administration.

This Act also makes technical corrections to conform existing law to the standards of the Delaware Legislative Drafting Manual.
