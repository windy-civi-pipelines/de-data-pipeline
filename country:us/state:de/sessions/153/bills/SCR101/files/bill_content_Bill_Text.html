WHEREAS, the American Civil War tested the nation's unity and commitment to equality, ultimately leading to the abolition of slavery and the expansion of civil rights; and

WHEREAS, the establishment of the National Parks System in 1916 reflected a collective commitment to preserving the natural beauty and environmental heritage of the country; and
