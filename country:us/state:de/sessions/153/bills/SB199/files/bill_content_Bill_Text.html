153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY

SENATE BILL NO. 199

AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 26 OF THE DELAWARE CODE AND CHAPTER 44 OF VOLUME 85 OF THE LAWS OF DELAWARE RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF DELAWARE:

Section 1. Amend Section 3 of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware by deleting Section 3 of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware in its entirety and inserting in lieu thereof the following:

âSection 3. This Act takes effect on January 31, 2026.â.

Section 2. This Act takes effect retroactively as of the enactment of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware.

Section 3. This Act does not affect or limit the retroactive effect of Section 2 of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware.

SYNOPSIS

This Act amends Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware, formerly Senate Bill No. 159, as amended by Senate Amendment No. 1 and House Amendment No. 1, of the 153rd General Assembly, to delay the effective date of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware until January 31, 2026, and to repeal the sunset clause in Section of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware.

This Act is retroactive to the enactment of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware.

This Act does not affect or limit the retroactive effect of Section 2 of Chapter 44 of Volume 85 of the Laws of Delaware.
